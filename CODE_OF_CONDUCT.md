# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
